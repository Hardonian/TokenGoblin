Frequently asked questions

What counts as a token?

All input and output tokens processed by LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, open-source models, etc.). We track per-call token counts from your usage data.

Can I start free and upgrade later?

Absolutely. Start with the free tier, and when you need more capacity or features, upgrade to Pro or Enterprise seamlessly via Stripe.

How is pricing calculated for overages?

Pro includes 500K tokens/month. Additional tokens are billed at $0.10/1K tokens. Enterprise includes unlimited tokens.

Do you offer annual plans at a discount?

Yes — annual billing is available at 2 months free. Contact us to set it up.
